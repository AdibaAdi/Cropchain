## F. Assumptions and Open Questions

### Assumptions
- All actors will have access to the internet and necessary devices to use the platform.
- The technology stack chosen will support the required functionalities.
- Stakeholders will be available for feedback and testing during the sprint.

### Open Questions
- What specific metrics should be included in the yield predictions?
- How will the alert system be integrated with existing communication channels?
- Are there any specific regulations regarding data handling that need to be addressed in the initial development phase?
